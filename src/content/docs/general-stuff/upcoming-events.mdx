### Network tab

While visiting the events page on the Sandbox website at [](https://www.sandbox.game/en/events/), have a look at your browser dev tool's network tab and search for an entry called *social-events?bannerVisualization[]=events...*.


### Raw JSON

Open [](https://api.sandbox.game/social-events?bannerVisualization[]=events&upcoming=true&past=true&limit=10&offset=0&available=true) and read the JSON manually.
