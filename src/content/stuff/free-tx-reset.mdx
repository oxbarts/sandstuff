## How to find out WEN your free transactions will reset manually

So you wanna know when your 10 free monthly transactions will be reset and don't want to use my tool? Here you go!

1. On the Sandbox website, click on your profile pic and navicate to **Settings > Wallet**. This link will also take you to the <a href="https://www.sandbox.game/en/me/settings/blockchain/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wallet Settings</a>.
2. Press <kbd>F12</kbd> to open Chrome DevTools and go to the **Network** tab.

   ![](@/assets/devtools-network-tab.webp)

   The Network tab sometimes hides behind the double arrow <ChevronsRight className="size-4 inline-block"/>. Just click on it and you'll find the tab.
3. Type <kbd>account</kbd> in the Filter text box and select Fetch/XHR, then reload the page.
4. Next, click the entry names **check-limit/?account=general** and you'll find the reset time as timestamp (milliseconds sicne Jan 1st, 1970, UTC). You can translate that to a human-readable format with a timestamp converter, that you can find with your favorite search engine.
   ![](@/assets/devtools-check-limit.webp)
